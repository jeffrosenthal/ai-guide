What Is AI, Really?

No buzzwords. Just a straight answer.

Artificial Intelligence is software that can understand language, answer questions, write content, analyze information, and help you think through problems ‚Äî the same way a brilliant colleague might, except it's available 24 hours a day, never gets tired, and can process information faster than any human.

What Is an AI Agent?

The difference between AI that answers and AI that acts.

ü§î Basic AI
